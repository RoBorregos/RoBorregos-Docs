# General Construction

The robot structure was designed prioritizing mechanical resistance and proper mass distribution. The black structural components were manufactured using **ABS**, while the white secondary components were manufactured using **PLA**.

The primary objective was to maximize the use of ABS due to its superior rigidity and impact resistance during competition. This allowed the robot to better withstand collisions and mechanical stress generated during gameplay.

| Component | Material | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Black parts | ABS | Higher structural resistance |
| White parts | PLA | Secondary components |
